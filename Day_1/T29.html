Both the <i> and <em> tags are used to italicize text, but they serve different purposes. The <i> tag is typically used for styling text in a different voice or tone, such as technical terms, foreign words, or thoughts. On the other hand, the <em> tag is used to give emphasis to words that are important in the context, and screen readers will also recognize this added meaning. So while both appear italicized by default, <em> has semantic value, whereas <i> is more presentational.
