Chocolate Cookie

Ingridients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix butter and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix well.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir to combine.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips.
  5. Make small dough balls and place on tray.
  6. Bake at 180°C for 10–12 minutes.
