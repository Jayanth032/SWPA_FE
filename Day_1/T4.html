Air India Boeing 787 Crashes Shortly After Takeoff from Ahmedabad!

Tragedy Strikes Near Medical College Hostel

The flight, AI‑171 bound for London Gatwick, crashed into the BJ Medical College hostel moments after takeoff on June 12, 2025.

Catastrophic Loss: Over 270 Lives Lost

With 242 passengers and crew onboard, 241 perished; at least 38 residents on the ground were also killed, bringing the total death toll to around 279

Miracle Survivor Emerges

Only one passenger survived: British‑Indian national Vishwash Kumar Ramesh, seated at 11A, who walked away from the wreckage and is now recovering in hospital

First Insights from Investigation

Black Box Retrieval and Preliminary Analysis

Both flight data and cockpit voice recorders have been recovered. Authorities are scrutinizing possible engine thrust loss, landing‑gear issues, and dual‑engine failure
