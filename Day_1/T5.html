Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Author

J.K. Rowling

Published Year

1997

Story Summary

Harry Potter is an orphan who finds out on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard. He goes to Hogwarts, a school for magic, and makes friends. He learns about his past and faces danger to stop an evil wizard named Voldemort.

Main Characters

  • Harry Potter
  • Hermione Granger
  • Ron Weasley
  • Albus Dumbledore
  • Lord Voldemort

Important Themes

  • Friendship
  • Bravery
  • Good vs Evil

Fun Fact

In the U.S., the book is called Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone.
