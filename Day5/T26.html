How to Make Fried Rice

  1. Cook rice ahead of time and let it cool (preferably use day-old rice for best texture).
  2. Heat oil in a large wok or pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add chopped garlic and sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add diced onions, carrots, and other vegetables of your choice. Stir-fry until tender.
  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and crack in 1–2 eggs. Scramble until fully cooked.
  6. Add the cooled rice to the pan and mix everything together.
  7. Pour in soy sauce, a pinch of salt, and a dash of pepper. Stir well to coat the rice evenly.
  8. Optional: Add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for extra protein.
  9. Stir-fry for another 2–3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  10. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot!
